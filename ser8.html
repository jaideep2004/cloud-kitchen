Staff Training

We don't
just train.
We certify.

Hiring is only half the job. Our dedicated in-house chefs travel to your kitchen — city, state, or country — and train your entire team until every dish meets the benchmark. Then we sign off.

01
Dedicated In-House Chef Deployment
A qualified chef from our payroll — 8–10 years hotel management experience — is assigned to you personally and travels to your kitchen, wherever it is.
02
Complete SOP & Recipe Training
Step-by-step recipe execution, portion control, plating standards, and daily operational SOPs — all taught through live practical demonstrations.
03
Multiple Food Trials & Practical Learning
Repeated hands-on cooking trials for every dish. Real-time corrections on taste, texture, presentation, and speed — until mastery is achieved.
04
Final Quality Checks & Staff Certification
Individual performance checks per chef, dish-wise quality validation, and final approval only when output matches the planned menu benchmarks.
