What Is This

Menu Engineering
& Why It Matters.

Menu engineering is the science of analyzing your dishes based on two core dimensions: Profitability (food cost vs. selling price) and Popularity (order frequency & demand).

A menu is not just a list of dishes — it's your primary revenue engine. We engineer menus that maximize average order value (AOV), contribution margins, and repeat customer rates.
