Section 06
Cancellations & Refunds

Due to the perishable nature of food, our cancellation and refund policy is as follows:

  1. Free Cancellation: Orders may be cancelled within 2 minutes of placement for a full refund.
  2. Partial Refund: If your order has already begun preparation, a partial refund may be issued at our discretion.
  3. No Cancellation: Orders that are out for delivery cannot be cancelled.
  4. Wrong Order / Missing Items: Contact us within 30 minutes of delivery for resolution.
  5. Quality Issues: Report food quality concerns with photographic evidence within 1 hour of delivery.
💰

Approved refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method or as Cloud Kitchen credits.

We reserve the right to refuse refund requests that we deem fraudulent or abusive. Repeated refund claims may result in account suspension.
