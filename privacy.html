Section 01
Overview & Commitment

Welcome to My Cloud Kitchen's Privacy Policy. We operate the website at my-cloud-kitchen.onrender.com and are deeply committed to safeguarding the privacy and personal information of everyone who interacts with our platform.

🔒

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and what rights you have over your information. We never sell your personal data to third parties — ever.

This policy applies to all personal information collected through our website, mobile application, customer service interactions, and any other services offered by My Cloud Kitchen. By using our services, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this policy.

We comply with applicable data protection laws including India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP Act), 2023 and other relevant regulations. This policy is reviewed and updated regularly to reflect any changes in our practices or applicable law.

If you have any concerns about how we handle your data, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@mycloudkitchen.com at any time.

📥
Section 02
Information We Collect

We collect information in three main ways: information you provide directly, information collected automatically, and information from third parties.

Information You Provide Directly:

  • Full name and contact details (email address, phone number)
  • Delivery address and location data
  • Account credentials (username, encrypted password)
  • Payment information (processed via secure third-party gateways)
  • Order history, preferences, and dietary requirements
  • Reviews, feedback, and communications you submit
  • Profile photo and any optional profile information

Information Collected Automatically:

  • IP address and approximate geolocation
  • Browser type, version, and operating system
  • Device identifiers and mobile device type
  • Pages visited, time spent, and clickstream data
  • Referring website or source of traffic
  • Session duration and interaction patterns
ℹ️

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our services effectively. You may use certain features of our platform without providing all information, though this may limit the services available to you.

Section 04
Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We share your data only in the limited circumstances described below, and always with appropriate safeguards in place.

We may share your data with:

  • Delivery partners — Your name, address, and contact number are shared with our delivery personnel solely to fulfil your order
  • Payment processors — Secure third-party gateways such as Razorpay, PayU, or Stripe process your payment information under their own strict privacy standards
  • Cloud & IT service providers — Hosting, database, and infrastructure providers who process data on our behalf under data processing agreements
  • Analytics providers — Aggregated, anonymised data may be shared with analytics services to help us improve our platform
  • Legal authorities — When required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights and safety of our users and business
  • Business successors — In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred as part of the transaction, subject to continued privacy protections
✅

All third parties who receive your data are contractually required to handle it securely and only for the specified purpose. They are prohibited from using your data for their own marketing.

Section 05
Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyse site traffic, and personalise content. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.

Types of cookies we use:

Cookie Type Purpose Status
Essential Login sessions, cart persistence, security tokens Required
Functional Remembering preferences, language, and theme settings Required
Analytics Understanding page performance and user behaviour Optional
Marketing Personalised offers, retargeting, and ad measurement Optional

You can control cookies through your browser settings or our cookie preference centre. Disabling non-essential cookies will not affect core functionality, but may limit personalised features.

ℹ️

We also use local storage and session storage to maintain your cart and preferences across pages. These are cleared when you log out or clear your browser data.

Section 07
Data Security

Protecting your personal data is a top priority. We implement industry-standard technical and organisational security measures to prevent unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of your information.

Our security measures include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption — All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted in transit
  • Password hashing — Passwords are stored using strong one-way hashing algorithms (bcrypt) and are never stored in plain text
  • Access controls — Role-based access ensures only authorised personnel can access your data
  • Regular security audits — We conduct periodic vulnerability assessments and penetration testing
  • Two-factor authentication — Available for your account to add an extra layer of protection
  • Secure payment processing — We use PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways and do not store card numbers on our servers
  • Intrusion detection systems — Real-time monitoring for suspicious activity and anomalies
⚠️

No system is completely immune to security risks. In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and the relevant authorities within 72 hours of becoming aware.

Your Privacy Rights

You have significant rights over your personal data. Under applicable data protection law, you are entitled to the following:

👁️
Right to Access Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
✏️
Right to Rectification Correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data
🗑️
Right to Erasure Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
⏸️
Right to Restriction Limit how we process your data in certain circumstances
📦
Right to Portability Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
🚫
Right to Object Object to processing of your data for marketing or profiling
🤖
Automated Decisions Not be subject to solely automated decision-making that affects you
↩️
Right to Withdraw Consent Withdraw consent at any time where processing is consent-based

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@mycloudkitchen.com. We will respond to all valid requests within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling a request.

📋

Most of your rights can be exercised directly through your account dashboard under "Privacy Settings". For more complex requests, our privacy team is ready to assist.

Section 09
Children's Privacy

My Cloud Kitchen's services are not directed to, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from, children under the age of 13 years. Persons between the ages of 13 and 18 may only use our platform with verifiable parental or guardian consent.

⚠️

If we discover that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take immediate steps to delete that information. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

Parents and guardians are responsible for monitoring their children's online activities and use of our platform. If you are a parent or guardian and wish to review, delete, or restrict processing of your child's information, please contact us at privacy@mycloudkitchen.com.

Section 11
International Data Transfers

My Cloud Kitchen primarily stores and processes your data within India. However, some of our third-party service providers may be located in other countries, which may result in your data being transferred internationally.

Whenever we transfer personal data outside India, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

Section 12
Marketing & Communications

With your consent, we may send you promotional communications about our offers, new menu items, discounts, and service updates. You have full control over the communications you receive from us.

Types of communications we may send:

  • Transactional — Order confirmations, delivery updates, receipts (cannot be opted out of while you have an active account)
  • Service notifications — Account security alerts, policy updates, important platform changes
  • Promotional emails — Special offers, seasonal menus, discount codes (requires explicit consent)
  • SMS notifications — Delivery tracking, OTP verification (required for service), promotional SMS (optional)
  • Push notifications — Order updates and offers via our app (controlled via device settings)
📧

You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email, or by updating your notification preferences in your account settings. We will process your request within 10 business days.

Even after opting out of marketing communications, you will continue to receive essential transactional messages related to active orders and account security.
