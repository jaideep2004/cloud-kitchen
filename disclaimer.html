Allergen Disclaimer

My Cloud Kitchen takes food allergies and intolerances extremely seriously. We provide allergen information on product pages to the best of our ability. However, the following limitations apply to all allergen-related information:

🚨

Cross-contamination risk: Our kitchen prepares food containing all 14 major allergens recognised under food safety regulations, including nuts, dairy, gluten, eggs, soy, fish, and shellfish. We cannot guarantee the complete absence of any allergen in any dish, even those labelled as "allergen-free".

Allergens present in our kitchen environment include:

  • Tree nuts (almonds, cashews, walnuts, pistachios, etc.)
  • Peanuts and peanut-based products
  • Dairy products (milk, cheese, butter, cream, ghee)
  • Gluten-containing grains (wheat, barley, rye)
  • Eggs and egg-derived ingredients
  • Fish and fish-derived sauces or stocks
  • Shellfish and crustaceans
  • Soy and soy-based products
  • Sesame seeds and sesame oil
  • Mustard and mustard-derived products

My Cloud Kitchen shall not be held liable for any allergic reaction, health complication, or adverse event resulting from consumption of our products where the customer failed to disclose relevant allergen information prior to placing an order. Customers with life-threatening allergies are strongly advised to contact us directly before ordering to discuss their requirements.
