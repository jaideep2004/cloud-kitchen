Earning Potential

Minimum investment.
Maximum return.

Unlike traditional franchise agreements, becoming a Hustlers Hospitality partner requires no heavy upfront fees. To ensure partners are serious, we charge a one-time setup and onboarding fee per food concept category.

The amount of profits you earn depends on how many Hustlers Hospitality concepts you sign up for. Each concept earns you, on average, between ₹20,000 to ₹25,000 per month.

Because you already pay for rent, equipment, and staff salaries regardless — these costs are already covered. Only additional food costs and aggregator commissions apply to the new brands.

How We Calculate

We only count additional food costs + delivery commissions against new brand revenue. Rent, equipment, and salaries are pre-sunk — making each cloud kitchen brand nearly pure incremental profit.
