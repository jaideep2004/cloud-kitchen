Cloud Kitchen Success Mantra

Questions every
new franchisee asks.

What makes a good brand listing on Zomato & Swiggy?

As a cloud kitchen, your real-time shop is how customers see you on delivery apps. A great listing drives conversions:

  • Creative, descriptive item names that stand out
  • Proper description of every food item
  • Professional food photography for each item
  • Varied add-ons and customisation options at selection
Is a professional food photoshoot required?

Absolutely yes. Professional food photography is one of the highest-ROI investments for a cloud kitchen. Our team provides photography guidelines and vendor support — items with professional photos consistently convert at 2–3x the rate of poorly-photographed listings.

Which offers, deals, and combos work best?

We use data analysis to determine the best discounting strategy, campaign participation, and cost-per-click approach for each brand and city. Our aggregator team manages this so you don't leave money on the table.

What is KPT and why does it matter?

KPT (Kitchen Preparation Time) is a critical ranking metric on Zomato and Swiggy. A low, consistent KPT improves your listing's position in search results. Our SOPs and kitchen layout design are specifically built to minimise KPT from day one.

What are the ideal operational hours for a cloud kitchen?

Cloud kitchens can legally operate 24 hours with proper FSSAI licensing and compliance. We recommend analysing your local demand curve — most kitchens see peak orders during lunch (12–2 PM), dinner (7–10 PM), and late-night (12–3 AM). We guide you to optimise staffing for peak windows to maximise profitability.

How important is social media and digital marketing?

Critical for building brand recall and driving direct orders. Our digital marketing support covers targeted social media campaigns based on local demography, content strategy, and performance analysis — all included in our franchise package.

Is this franchise model profitable outside metro cities?

Absolutely. If you have the best food service, unique concepts, and superior quality, any city or location can be highly profitable. Our franchise partners in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities consistently outperform metro benchmarks because of lower rental costs and less delivery competition.
