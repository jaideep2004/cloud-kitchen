FAQs

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about the Accelerator Program before making your move.

What is the eligibility criteria?
We partner with successful F&B brands that are live on delivery apps, currently operating from a single kitchen or location, and have a proven concept with strong ratings. Your cuisine must be replicable via a standardised SOP across multiple kitchens.
How many kitchens can we be live in?
We can get your brand live at 20+ kitchen locations across our existing pan-India network in under 30 days. The exact count depends on your cuisine type and city availability within our network.
What is the investment for the growth hack model?
We invest our systems, chef teams, supply chain, and kitchen infrastructure — you don't pay rent or hire new staff. The partnership model is based on a revenue-sharing arrangement. Specific terms are discussed during onboarding. Get in touch for a detailed discussion.
How will this be beneficial to my brand?
You go from 1 location and ~200 orders to 20+ locations and 4000+ orders — increasing both your revenue and your brand's market presence and valuation by up to 20×. Multi-city presence also makes your brand significantly more attractive for funding, franchise, or acquisition.
Will my food quality & taste remain the same?
Absolutely. This is our highest priority. We invest significant time in developing and documenting Recipe SOPs with you, and our chef teams are fully trained on your recipes before going live. Taste consistency across every location is non-negotiable.
Do I have to pay salaries and rent?
No. That's the core advantage of the model. We provide the kitchen space, equipment, chef teams, and supply chain. You bring your brand, recipes, and entrepreneurial energy. There is no rent or salary outgo on your end for the new locations.
